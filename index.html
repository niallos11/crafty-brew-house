What we are all About


We are club for Craft Beer & Homebrew enthusiasts throughout all of Ireland.

The club is all about teaching and learning about craft beers & homebrewing

Our social club offers a great way to meetup fellow Brewers to share ideas and taste all the best craft beers available throughout Ireland.


Why Join


Join our club and you will receive;

- Weekly newsletters with information on latest craft beers & homebrew equipment.

- Tips and meetups from our team of Master Brewers.

- Discounts on craft beer & equipment for suppliers nationwide.

- Free sample packs supplied from our craft beer partners.

- Discounted Micro brewery tours for all out members
